seo ands forSearch Engine Optimization . It is the practice of optimizing a website in order to improve its ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs). SEO helps to increase the quality and quantity of website traffic by making the website more visible in search engines. There are many different aspects to SEO, including the use of keywords, the creation of high-quality content, and the optimization of website structure and code. SEO is important because it helps to make a website more visible to potential customers, which can lead to increased traffic, sales, and revenue.
